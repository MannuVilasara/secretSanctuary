SecretSanctary is an anonymous confession, feedback, and suggestions bot for Discord. It allows users to share their thoughts anonymously, ensuring their privacy and confidentiality.

How It Works

1. Add SecretSanctary bot to your Discord server.

2. Users can submit their confessions, feedback, or suggestions using the bot's commands.

3. All submissions are kept anonymous and private.

Privacy Protection

SecretSanctary bot takes user privacy seriously. All submissions are encrypted and stored securely. No personal information is collected or shared.
